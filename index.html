Group Lunch Picker

When the collective mind can't decide what takeout to choose

Group Lunch Picker helps you and your friends decide what to eat. Add your favorite foods to the list, spin the wheel, and let chance decide. The app ensures no food is repeated until every other option has been picked, keeping things fair and fun.
